About Adegan95

Transforming the world into an abundant, edible, safe natural ecosystem.

Our Purpose

Adegan95 Global Enterprise is driven by a bold mission: to return the Earth to a natural state where nature freely provides for mankind. We believe that cities, villages and farms can all become living ecosystems overflowing with edible trees, non‑toxic flowers, and harmonious animals that enrich life.

Our Vision

We envision a future where streets are lined with fruit-bearing trees, farms are designed to prioritize community nourishment, and every environment supports life without harm. Imagine parks filled with edible flowers, public spaces that feed people, and animals that coexist peacefully with humans.

Core Values

  • Restoration of natural abundance
  • Sustainability and long-term stewardship
  • Community empowerment and education
  • Innovation in agriculture and nature design
  • Harmony between humans, plants, and animals
