Our Projects

Building an edible, harmonious world—one community at a time.

Street Pantry Initiative

Transforming streets into food corridors by replacing ornamental trees with fruit-bearing varieties. These edible streets produce shade, food, and beauty—serving communities naturally and sustainably.

Edible Schoolyards

Turning school environments into learning orchards. Children grow, harvest, and taste real food while learning about nature, sustainability, and healthy living.

Edible Flower & Herb Program

Researching and cultivating non-toxic, edible flowers and herbs. These plants are integrated into streets, parks, and gardens to provide both beauty and nourishment.

Harmonious Animal Stewardship

Encouraging communities to raise and support safe, beneficial animals that do not harm humans or the ecosystem. This improves biodiversity and community well-being.

Seed & Knowledge Bank

An open-source library of seeds and educational materials that empower communities to grow, plant, and duplicate edible ecosystems anywhere in the world.

Plastic Production & Manufacturing

Production and manufacturing of plastics of all kinds and applications, including edible plastics, wearable plastics, electronics components, and other innovative uses.
